## Part 1 — Privacy Policy + Terms of Service

Both Apple and Google **require** a hosted privacy policy before they'll approve your app. This is a hard blocker.

### Option A — Fast (Recommended for launch)
Use a privacy policy generator:
- https://www.privacypolicies.com/privacy-policy-generator/ (free)
- https://app-privacy-policy-generator.nisrulz.com/ (free, app-specific)

Key things your privacy policy must mention for DoorDrill:
- You collect: name, email address, audio recordings, session performance data
- Audio recordings are processed by third-party AI providers (OpenAI, Deepgram, ElevenLabs)
- Data is stored securely and not sold to third parties
- Users can request deletion of their data

### Option B — Host it yourself
Create a simple page at a URL like `doordrill.com/privacy` or use Notion with a public page.

**The URL must be reachable without a login.** A Notion public page works fine.

## Part 3 — Screenshots

You need screenshots for both iOS and Android. The easiest way is to use the Expo Go simulator.

### iOS Screenshot Sizes Required
- **6.9" display** (iPhone 16 Pro Max): 1320×2868 px — required
- **12.9" iPad Pro**: 2048×2732 px — required if your app supports iPad

### Android Screenshot Sizes
- Any 16:9 or 9:16 ratio, min 320px on shortest side

### Suggested 5 Screenshots (same for both platforms)
1. **Login / Onboarding** — "AI-Powered D2D Sales Training"
2. **Active Drill Session** — microphone active, conversation in progress
3. **Score Screen** — showing a completed session scorecard (6.5+ score with category breakdown)
4. **Manager Dashboard** — rep list with performance metrics
5. **Assignment / Progress** — rep's training history or leaderboard

### How to Take Them
```bash
cd mobile
npx expo start
# Open in iOS Simulator → Device → Screenshot (Cmd+S saves to Desktop)
# For exact resolution, use: Simulator → Window → Physical Size
```
